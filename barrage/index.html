# Video barrage

MuiPlayer has not yet developed its own barrage function, but you can still use the three-party barrage plug-in, and you can retrieve many open source and excellent video barrage projects (opens new window) from github. E.g:

  • https://github.com/pprory/easyDanmaku (opens new window)
  • https://github.com/jabbany/CommentCoreLibrary (opens new window)
